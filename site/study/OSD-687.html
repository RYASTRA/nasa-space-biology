OSD-687 · GLDS-618 · released 2024-05-14

SpaceX Inspiration4 T-Cell Histone Modification Profiling (H3K4me1, H3K4me3, H3K27ac)

The SpaceX Inspiration4 mission was a 3-day mission with four private astronauts to low Earth orbit that occurred in September 2021. T-cells were isolated from whole blood in one crew member at the R+194 timepoint for histone modification quantification as a proof of concept study, as no histone modifications have been previously studied in astronaut cohorts. The H3K4me1, H3K4me3, and H3K27ac modifications were profiled using Cut and Run.

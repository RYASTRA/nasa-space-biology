OSD-214 · GLDS-214 · released 2019-02-15

Impact of Antiorthostatic Suspension on Mouse response to Tetanus Toxoid and CpG: bone marrow transcriptomic data

These data were generated from mice that were skeletally unloaded using antiorthostatic suspension (AOS). Mice were either subjected to AOS or tail restraint without unloading for two weeks before injection with saline +/- tetanus toxoid +/- Cpg. Mice were unloaded for an additional two weeks before being sacrificed. This set of data focused on bone marrow. The spleen data are available in the GLDS-201.

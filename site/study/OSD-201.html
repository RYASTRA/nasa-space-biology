OSD-201 · GLDS-201 · released 2018-09-21

Impact of Antiorthostatic Suspension on Mouse response to Tetanus Toxoid and CpG

These data were generated from mice that were skeletally unloaded using antiorthostatic suspension (AOS). Mice were either subjected to AOS or tail restraint without unloading for two weeks before injection with saline +/- tetanus toxoid +/- Cpg. Mice were unloaded for an additional two weeks before being sacrificed.
